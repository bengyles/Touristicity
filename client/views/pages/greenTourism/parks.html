Le Canal de la Siagne - Mandelieu

Characteristics

  • Distance : 4,6km
  • Time : 2h
  • Level : easy

Access for le Cannet

  • Car : Located in Cannet at 4km from the city center, take avenue Ziem and park at one of the parking lots
  • Bus : Take bus n°1 (Palm Bus), stop « Bonnard » and walk 25 minutes, take la rue St Sauveur and the stairs St Antoine (240 stairs)
  • Train : Stop « Gare de Cannes », bus n°1 (Palm Bus) and walk 25 minutes, take la rue St Sauveur and take the stairs St Antoine

Access for Mougins

  • Car : Located in Mougins, 7km from the city center of Cannes
  • Bus : Take bus n°630 ( Lignes d’Azur), stop « Qui vend bon »
  • Train : Stop « Gare de Cannes » , and take bus n°630 (Ligne d’Azur)
